I am a Computer Science graduate from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology with a strong interest in **Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Data Science**.

My work focuses on **Bangla text analysis**, including sentiment analysis, hate speech detection, and review mining. I have experience applying both **traditional machine learning models (XGBoost, SVM, Naïve Bayes, KNN)** and **deep learning approaches such as Transformer-based models and TextCNN** for text classification tasks.

I enjoy building **end-to-end ML pipelines**, from data preprocessing and feature engineering to model training, evaluation, and deployment. I am particularly interested in developing practical ML solutions that extract insights from real-world textual data.

My long-term goal is to work as a **Data Scientist or AI Engineer**, contributing to impactful machine learning applications and research.
